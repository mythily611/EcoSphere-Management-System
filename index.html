About EcoSphere

EcoSphere is an ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) Management Platform designed to help organizations improve sustainability, employee engagement, and governance compliance.

The platform provides real-time ESG monitoring, carbon emission tracking, CSR management, governance reporting, and gamification features to encourage employee participation in sustainability initiatives.
